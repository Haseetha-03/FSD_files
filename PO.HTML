Powdery Mildew (Crop)

White, powdery spots on leaves and stems. Common in many crops.

Fin Rot (Fish)

Frayed or deteriorating fins, often with red or white edges.

Fowl Pox (Poultry)

Warty lesions on comb, wattles, and unfeathered skin.

Bacterial Blight (Crop)

Water-soaked spots that turn brown/black, often with yellow halos.

Pebrine (Silkworm)

Black spots on silkworms, poor growth, and irregular molting.

Varroa Mites (Bee)

Parasitic mites that weaken bee colonies and transmit viruses.
