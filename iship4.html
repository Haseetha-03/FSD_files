At AgriAssist, we believe in empowering farmers with knowledge, tools, and hope for a better tomorrow.

🌿 Our Journey

It all began under a humble mango tree, where a group of agriculture enthusiasts and techies saw a recurring pattern — farmers struggling due to lack of real-time guidance, fair market access, and disease awareness. That day, a seed was planted.

AgriAssist grew from that seed — into a platform that blends technology with tradition, offering region-focused solutions to help farmers thrive in an ever-changing world.

🚜 Our Mission

“To revolutionize agriculture in Visakhapatnam and beyond by delivering data-powered insights, local support, and an inclusive farming community.”
